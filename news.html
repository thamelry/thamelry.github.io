News

  • 17/04/2023: Ola Rønning successfully defends his PhD. Ola worked on deep generative models of protein structure and Stein variational inference for Bayesian deep models. Funding came from the Independent Research Fund Denmark. Co-supervisor was Assoc. Prof. Christophe Ley, University of Luxemburg
  • 13/03/2023: Thomas becomes Senior Associate Editor of the new journal ACM Transactions on Probabilistic Machine Learning (TOPML).
  • 04/01/2023: Christian Thygesen, Industrial PhD student with Evaxion, successfully defends his PhD. Christian worked on deep generative models of protein structure (published in ICML, 2021), with applications in vaccine design.
  • 08/09/2022: Thomas is awarded a Villum EXPERIMENT grant ("Predicting protein structure, mutations and dynamics with deep generative models", DKK 1,8 million.)
  • 22/04/2022: Lys Sanz Moreta successfully defends her PhD. Lys worked on deep generative models of protein evolution, with applications in ancestral sequence reconstruction (published in ICLR, 2022).
